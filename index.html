Cosmology results

Here is a protopyte of an interactive page showing chains outputs (2D contours at the moment) from cosmological experiments. The goal is to easily explore cosmological results without having to plot chains yourself, or open and scroll through different papers. This prototype is meant to probe the interest of the communnity in such a tool, let me know your thoughts (agnes.ferte@jpl.nasa.gov). You would be able to select many more experiments, cosmological parameters and chain outputs (e.g. 1D contour, tension metrics, etc) in the final product.

Try it out by checking the boxes below to specify the combinations of cosmological parameters you are interested in and the cosmological experiment (DES Y3 and Planck 2018 (using DES Y3 priors) at the moment), to show the 2D contour plot.

Written by Agnès Ferté, using the figure-pager package written by Justin Willmert here, a plotting script written by Jessie Muir for DES, using CosmoSIS.
